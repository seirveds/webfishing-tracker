How to Use Fish Tracker

Track your fishing progress by marking the quality of the fish you caught.

Click the colored squares mark a fish as caught for that quality level. Click it again to unmark it.

Additional Features:

  • Use Quick Options at the bottom of the page to mark all fish in a category
  • Click the fish for detailed information
  • Check your completion progress in the Completion tab
